About.

I love designing things but I also love seeing my designs work. Ideas are only so much without implementation, so I move around design and development at will.

I also like to tell stories and document things, whether its with a photograph, a video, or a 3D image.

In the end, I want my work to touch people, through emotion and function. That's the final step of the process, starting with designing concepts, then bringing those concepts to life with code, and then finally changing people's lives with the final result.

That's what I want to do. I may not be there now, but I'll improve my skills piece by piece. Design and development are not instantaneous processes, and neither are people's lives.
