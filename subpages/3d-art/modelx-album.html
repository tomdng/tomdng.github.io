Low-Poly Tesla Model X

12/19/2017

After my first animation, I wanted to work with a short of "fun" project with a little more freedom as to what I wanted to create. This time I wanted to actually be able to fully model a car (last time I tried I only got to the hood of the car). In the midst of a busy schedule this animation took my three months, from idea to the finished video, to create, and was ready by the date you see above. I wanted to do a lot more with the surrounding landscape and polish the animation and some of the camera movements so that they looked more natural, but making this was essential for me to recognize what I could do better for my next animation, which is particularly attention to details. Anways, enjoy the album!
